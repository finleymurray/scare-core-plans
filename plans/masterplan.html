02 — Headquarters
The Sector Hub — Detailed Floor Plan
1,500m² purpose-built headquarters for Immersive Core. Steel frame with insulated cladding, 6m eaves height to the rehearsal space, 3.5m standard ceiling elsewhere. The building is divided into four functional zones: administration, production, costume/wardrobe, and the black box rehearsal studio.
Building 01
The Sector Hub — Ground Floor
GIA: 1,500m² Footprint: 50m × 30m Height: 3.5m (offices) / 6m (rehearsal) Structure: Steel Portal Frame Budget: £1,800,000
Construction: Steel portal frame, insulated composite cladding, concrete slab floor (power-floated in workshop, raised access in offices). Standing seam metal roof with 10% rooflights. Services: 3-phase power throughout, gas heating to offices, workshop extract ventilation, sprinkler system, fire alarm (L2), intruder alarm, Cat6A network cabling, NFC door access on all zones.
03 — Maze Structures
Maze Barn Design
Six purpose-built steel-frame barns arranged around The Yard. Each barn is a blank canvas: a clear-span shell that the creative team fits out with modular wall systems, scenic elements, lighting rigs, and audio. The barn design allows complete reconfiguration between seasons without structural modification.
Buildings 02-07
Typical Maze Barn — Sections & Layout
GIA: 850–950m² Footprint: 30m × 30m (typical) Eaves: 5.5m clear Structure: Clear-Span Steel Portal Budget: £600k shell + £500k fit-out
Maze wall system: Modular steel-stud partition framework (SFS) with 18mm OSB sheathing both sides, allowing scenic attachment via screws. Walls at 2.7m standard height with 3.0m feature walls. False ceilings at 2.5m using fabric/timber frames. Services per barn: 48-way DMX lighting grid, 8-zone audio (individual speakers per scene), 4 fog machines, 2 scent machines, emergency lighting (3hr maintained), fire detection (beam detectors above false ceiling), 2 fire exits (illuminated, panic hardware). Acoustic: 50mm mineral wool insulation in all partition walls to prevent sound bleed between scenes.

04 — Central Hub
The Yard — Courtyard Hub
The social heart of Scare Core. An open-air courtyard surrounded by the maze barns, featuring themed food and drink, merchandise, live entertainment, and queue management. Designed to feel like a dystopian industrial compound that guests explore between mazes.
Zone 01
The Yard — Courtyard Layout
Area: ~1,200m² open courtyard Capacity: 400 standing Covered: 2× canopy structures F&B: Main bar + 2 satellite points
Atmosphere: Low-level theatrical lighting throughout (no standard floodlights). Permanent PA system for music/announcements. 4 fog machines on timers creating ground-level haze. Fire pit (gas, controlled) as central feature. Themed scenic dressing: industrial signage, warning barriers, dystopian props. Covered structures: Two 10m × 6m steel canopy frames with stretched fabric roofing, open sides, festoon lighting. Protects seated guests from rain.
05 — Outdoor Spaces
Landscape & External Areas
The 52 acres beyond the guest core provide essential buffer landscaping, parking, drainage, and future expansion space. The landscape strategy serves both practical (screening, drainage, ecological mitigation) and atmospheric (approach experience, isolation) purposes.

07 — Guest Journey
From Car Park to Final Scare
The entire guest experience is designed as a single continuous narrative arc, from the moment they park their car through to the final scare. Every transition point is an opportunity to build tension, excitement, or relief.
1
Arrive & Park
750-space car park. First glimpse of atmospheric lighting beyond the treeline. Audio begins.
2
The Approach
200m walk through landscaped corridor. Low fog, distant screams, themed signage builds dread.
3
Entrance Gate
Ticket scan, bag check, waiver. In-character staff. The threshold into the world.
4
The Yard
Central courtyard. Bar, food, merch, stage. Roaming actors. Queue for first maze.
5
Maze Queue
Themed queue experience with in-world lore, audio, and minor scares. ~20 min at peak.
6
The Maze
10-14 min immersive experience. 7-9 scenes, 8-14 actors, full sensory assault.
7
Decompression
Exit into The Yard. Photo station. Return to bar. Queue for next maze. Repeat ×6.
8
Departure
Merch on exit. Survivor's Log QR code. Atmospheric walk back to car park.
