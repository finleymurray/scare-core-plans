Rehearsal Space / Studio

Central Barn

The main central barn converted into a flexible rehearsal and studio space. Used day-to-day for actor training, choreography, scene blocking, and creative development. On event nights, part of this space converts to actor prep and a green room.

  • Main Studio FloorOpen rehearsal space, scene run-throughs
  • Choreography AreaMirrors, sprung floor (if feasible)
  • Creative DevelopmentStoryboarding, concept work, read-throughs

Event nights: Rear section converts to actor prep area (mirrors, final costume check, SFX touch-ups) and green room (rest area, refreshments, call board).

Future Phase — Entrance Plaza

The guest-facing entrance plaza, ticket windows, and park entry point will be developed as a future phase, connecting Core HQ (Lot 1) to the scare park worlds (Lot 2). This will sit to the south or south-east of the building cluster, providing a natural transition from the operational area to the guest experience. The plaza design will need to account for queue management, bag check, accessibility, and visual theming that sets the tone before guests enter the park.
